# Focus Guard

A minimal web-based productivity dashboard that combines a Pomodoro timer, task list, and camera detection to help you stay at your desk and keep your head in the game.

## Features

* **Local Vision Tracking:** Uses your webcam to monitor attention levels and count distractions locally—no video data ever leaves your machine.
* **Smart Pomodoro:** A structured workspace timer linked directly with the tracking state.
* **Task List:** Simple task tracking grouped by priority to organize your sessions.
* **Session Logs:** Local history showing completed sprints and distraction metrics.

## How to use:
* Click the link: https://ettihad.github.io/Focus-Guard/
* Grant camera permission
* Start your work
